Spatial operations on Simba

Simba (Spatial In-Memory Big data Analytics) extends the Spark SQL engine to provide rich spatial operations through both SQL and the DataFrame API. Besides, it introduces two-layer spatial indexing and cost-based optimizations to support efficient spatial queries in parallel. Simba is open source and public available at the project's webpage.

In particular, DISTANCE JOIN and CIRCLERANGE operators were used in order to find groups of points lying close enough each other.
